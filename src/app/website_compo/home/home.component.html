E-wish

This application will enable anyone who is expecting gifts from his Family, Relative and Friends on a special occasion like birthday, marriage ceremony or a graduation celebration according to his desire. Here the Gift Receiver and Gift Sender will use an automated well managed application.

Facility

This Application is to facilitate the users all over the world. The people who are keen to get gifts according to their desire and people who are keen to send their gifts to according to their beloved wishes would find our great blessing.

How it will work

User just needs to sign In after Sign Up, Create his E-wish (desire list of gift), Send it to his beloved. On the other hand other user, who received E-wish, will select the item and order it through Gift Vendor and send it to E-wish Creator.
